How to use eXo Platform 3 on your iPad

eXo Platform - Version 1.0

Login

Login: you must enter the URL where your instance of eXo Platform is located, along with your existing username and password

Language: change the display language.

Remember me: store your username and password for subsequent login attempts.

Auto login: bypass the login screen the next time you open the app.

After logging in, you will be redirected to the home screen of the app.

Sign Out button: touch on this button to sign out of Platform and go back to the Sign In page.

Chat

A list of other users available to chat will appear after tapping the Messenger [Chat] icon. Select a user, and a chat window will open.

Clear: remove the contents of the chat window currently in use

Close: return to the list of chat users.

Files

A list of available files in your eXo Platform repository will appear after tapping the Files icon.

Simply touch the file name to view an item.

To rename, delete, copy, cut or paste a file, select the "action" icon (the blue arrow) beside the item name.

Dashboard

Any gadgets you have previously added to your eXo Platform dashboard will appear here, as will any customizations or future changes to your dashboard.
