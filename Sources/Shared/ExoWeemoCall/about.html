About

This is a P.O.C of Weemo Video call integration in eXo iOS application. Weemo SDK needs at least iPhone 4s and iOS 5.1.

How it works

  • After authenticating to intranet, user is connected to Weemo cloud using his intranet account
  • To use video call, go to Home menu and select Call.
  • To call a user, select him in people list or enter manually his intranet account in Dial tab.
  • The people list is currently hard-coded. We are waiting for SOC-3848 to display user's connections.

Tips

  • You can check the connection status by checking the app log using free app Consolekit from App store

Known issues

  • With a bad connection, Weemo SDK often disconnects automatically. We have no way to catch this event (yet). When you can't call a user and there is no response from the app, please try to log out/kill the app and login again.
  • Probably because of connection quality, sometime the call is made but there is only voice, the video view is blank. Try to switch on/off the video, sometime it works.
